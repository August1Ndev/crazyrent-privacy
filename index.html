About This Policy

DIGITAL DREAM S.R.L. ("we", "our", or "the Company") operates the CrazyRent mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

CrazyRent is a business-to-business (B2B) tool used exclusively by DIGITAL DREAM S.R.L. employees and authorized personnel to manage vehicle rental contracts. It is not a public consumer application.

By using the CrazyRent application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The app collects and processes the following categories of information:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Authentication data Email address, password (hashed by Firebase) Secure login to the application
Contract data Contract number, rental period, advance payment Generating and managing rental contracts
Client personal data Full name, IDNP/IDNO, phone number, address, banking details Filling rental contract parties section
Vehicle data Brand, model, year, VIN, registration plate, daily cost, photo Fleet management and contract generation
Usage data App diagnostics (Firebase Analytics, crash reports) App stability and performance improvement
We do not collect payment card numbers, biometric data, or location data from app users. GPS data referenced in rental contracts is collected by the vehicle's GPS system, not this application.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and authorize access to the app
  • To create, store, and manage vehicle rental contracts
  • To generate PDF contracts for rental agreements
  • To maintain client and vehicle records for the rental business
  • To identify clients on a blacklist to prevent unauthorized rentals
  • To send password reset emails via Firebase Authentication
  • To improve app performance and fix technical issues

We process personal data on the legal basis of legitimate business interest and contractual necessity, in accordance with Moldovan Law No. 133 of July 8, 2011 on Personal Data Protection.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication with industry-standard security
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict database access to authenticated users only
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase handles all credential management
  • The app is available only to authorized company personnel

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Authentication data — retained for the duration of the authorized user account
  • Contract & client data — retained for a minimum of 5 years in accordance with Moldovan accounting and tax legislation
  • Vehicle data — retained while the vehicle is part of the active fleet

Upon request, we will delete personal data that is no longer legally required to be retained, within 30 days.

Your Rights

Under Moldovan Law No. 133/2011 and applicable EU data protection principles, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data where legally permissible
  • Restriction — request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
